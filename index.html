PHANTOM RECALL üß†

Under Review: ACL Rolling Review

Developed PHANTOM RECALL, a benchmark that exposes how LLMs rely on "remembered scripts" for classic puzzles even when internal logic is perturbed. Across 8 LLM families (GPT, Claude, LLaMA, etc.), we identified a recurring failure mode where models confidentially output standard solutions that no longer fit the scenario.

  • Automated Logic Judge: Engineered an automated judge achieving 96% human agreement in evaluating rationale validity.
  • Mitigation Prompts: Developed explicit "Assumption Forbidding" frameworks that boosted open-source model scores by up to +10.1 points.
  • Performance Gap: Identified that while "Thinking" models improve accuracy (GPT 28.8% to 60.4%), robustness to surface-level changes remains a critical unsolved gap.

Under Review: ACL Rolling Review

Introduced SporTabSet, a diagnostic benchmark for long-context (16k+ tokens) tabular summarization in sports. Evaluated how models maintain state and aggregate stats under complex, domain-specific rules (Basketball/Cricket).

L2 PERCEPTION & LIDAR üì°

The Digital Snowplow Pipeline

Developing a "Research Stack" architecture for autonomous vehicle navigation in whiteout conditions. This system moves away from heavy ROS 2 middleware to a lean, Python-native pipeline leveraging Open3D and Apple Metal (MPS) acceleration.

  • Digital Snowplow (L1): Implemented Statistical Outlier Removal (SOR) to filter weather noise by calculating mean neighbor distances, effectively "plowing" snow from the point cloud.
  • Temporal Cross-Attention (L2): Utilizing a Temporal Transformer that pulls "Historical Frames" via GPS metadata to compare snowy current frames with clear historical data.
  • Architecture: Upgraded traditional ResNet-18 backbones to Swin-Transformers to capture global context, targeting an 8% mAP increase in heavy snow conditions using the Ithaca365 dataset.
